Baby Ballon · 01/04

Mobile Game Design

Baby Ballon.

A social-awareness mobile game raising consciousness about "social giant babies." You play as the parent, facing real daily dilemmas. Three distinct endings based on your choices — designed, illustrated in Procreate, and user-tested end to end.

Tea Mate · 02/04

Physical Computing + UX

Tea Mate.

A smart tea table guiding users through Chinese tea culture step by step. Arduino sensors track brew weight and time, TouchDesigner plays instruction video via serial port, and QR payment is built in. From concept sketch to working physical prototype.

Two Brothers' Diary · 03/04

Interactive Book Design

Two Brothers' Diary.

A physical pop-up book combining NFC audio chips, AR overlays via Reality Composer, and Blender 3D modelling. Scan a page — hear audio. Push the slider — characters move. Led a multidisciplinary team from research to final product.

Musical Island · 04/04

Concept + Model + Branding

Musical Island.

A stress-relief island concept for people with sleep disorders — covering island zoning, sleep pod UX, a custom musical typeface, Procreate illustrations, and a physical foam model at 2400×1800mm. Data-driven research into global sleep disorders informed every decision.
